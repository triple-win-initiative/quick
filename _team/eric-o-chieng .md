Eric Ochieng is a Development Economist, Research Manager, and Researcher at Innovations for Poverty Action (IPA), and a Fall 2025 CEGA Fellow at the University of California, Berkeley. He works on the Kenya Life Panel Study (KLPS) and co-leads several RCTs in Kenya focusing on childcare, child development, agriculture, vaccination, and mental health. Eric has over 14 years of experience overseeing rigorous field experiments and is completing his PhD in Economics at Maseno University. His research centers on human capital investments, development economics, and health economics and aging.
